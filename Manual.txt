Registers:

    R0..R7    : General data registers
    PC        : Program counter
    ST        : Status
                C: Carry/Borrow
                Z: Result Zero
    
Instructions:

    LDI       : Load inmediate     LDI value, R(dst)
    LD        : Load mem           LD  MEM[R(src)], R(dst)
    
    ST        : Store mem          ST  R(src), MEM[R(dst)]
    
    ALU       : Alu operation      ALU op, R(src1), R(src2), R(dst)
